# SpaceLift Studio: Authority Website Blueprint

**Role**: Authority Website Architect  
**Goal**: Design the most authoritative, high-converting website architecture for SpaceLift Studio using competitor intelligence and B2B conversion design best practices.

---

## 1. Complete Sitemap

- **Home**: Technical authority landing page.
- **Solutions (The "How")**:
  - Digital Surface Manufacturing (Core Technical USP)
  - Experiential Fabrication
  - Large Format Event Printing
  - Environmental Graphics
- **Industries (The "Who")**:
  - Corporate & Brand Environments
  - Hospitality & Luxury Retail
  - Public & Cultural Spaces
  - Sports & Major Events
- **Portfolio (Case Studies)**: Filterable by Industry and Solution.
- **Sustainability**: Our commitment to green fabrication.
- **About**: Our Facility, Process, and Leadership.
- **Contact**: Project Discovery / Consultation.

---

## 2. Homepage Wireframe (Conversion Flow)

1.  **Hero Section**: High-impact video showing large-scale production + Technical Headline ("Digital Surface Manufacturing for Branded Environments").
2.  **Authority Bar (Trust)**: Immediate client logos (Social Proof).
3.  **The SpaceLift Edge**: High-speed statement on "National Infrastructure for Branded Environments."
4.  **Core Solutions Grid**: 4 cards highlighting fabrication expertise.
5.  **Industry Focus Segments**: Horizontal tabs or hover-reveal sections for different verticals.
6.  **Interactive Portfolio Highlight**: 3-4 marquee case studies.
7.  **Manufacturing Facility Tour (Visual)**: Proof of capability/scale. 
8.  **The Process (Timeline)**: Design -> Engineering -> Fabrication -> Installation.
9.  **Metric-Driven Proof**: "X Million SQ FT Produced", "Y Cities Served".
10. **Final CTA Section (High-Convert)**: "Request a Capabilities Review" or "Start Your Project Consultation."

---

## 3. Page-Level Architecture

### Core Solutions Pages
- **Purpose**: Technical SEO and buyer intent capture.
- **Required Sections**: Definition, Technical Specs, Material Library, Video of Fab Process, Related Case Studies.
- **Primary CTA**: Request Technical Specs / Quote.

### Industry Landing Pages
- **Purpose**: Vertical authority and resonance.
- **Required Sections**: Industry-Specific Challenges, Our Results (Visual), Testimonials, Vertical-Specific Client Logos.
- **Primary CTA**: Discuss Your Industry Needs.

---

## 4. Conversion Funnel Strategy

- **Primary CTA**: "Start Project Discussion" (High intent, low friction language).
- **Secondary CTA**: "Download 2026 Capabilities Deck" (Lead magnet for top-of-funnel).
- **Lead Capture**: Floating "Consultation" button + Exit-intent "Trending Fabrication Trends" popup.
- **Trust Elements**: Live "Availability Map" for national installs + Case Study "Deep Dives".

---

## 5. Components List

- **Navbar**: Glassmorphism, sticky with clear "Start Project" CTA.
- **Hero**: GSAP-animated type + high-bandwidth video background.
- **Solution Cards**: Hover-state reveals technical depth and materials.
- **Process Timeline**: Scroll-triggered animation showing physical build progress.
- **Metric Tiles**: Animated counters for scale.
- **Capability Deck Modal**: High-conversion lead capture gated content.
- **Industry Marquee**: Infinite scroll of vertical-specific logos.

---

## 6. Brand Positioning & Tone

- **Tone**: Technical, Confident, Architectural, Ultra-Clean.
- **Key Positioning**: SpaceLift is not a vendor; we are a **strategic manufacturing partner** providing the physical infrastructure for brand identity at scale.
